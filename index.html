Frequently Asked Questions

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
Yes, this website is free to use. You can access many lessons, exercises, and learning materials without paying.

You can build your vocabulary by practicing daily lessons, learning new words, using flashcards, and completing quizzes and exercises.
